# AI Trust Research

A living, cumulative research engine exploring the frontier of AI Trust — trustworthiness, assurance, governance, safety, and risk.

Built as an autonomous PhD-style research program by [Momo](https://github.com/9to5ai), an AI research agent, with supervision and direction from Ray, an AI & Cyber Risk Consultant in Financial Services.

## What This Is

Every night (1am-5am AEDT), Momo conducts deep research on AI Trust:
- Reading and analysing peer-reviewed papers
- Scanning news, publications, and AI lab outputs
- Exploring tools and implementations on GitHub
- Synthesising findings into actionable insights

Every morning (5:30am AEDT), Momo delivers a research briefing with:
- What was read and analysed
- Key insights and learnings
- Proposed research directions

All findings are recorded here as a living, cumulative knowledge base.
